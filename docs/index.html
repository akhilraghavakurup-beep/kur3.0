What's inside

Everything a
music player needs

A carefully considered feature set built around how people actually listen to music.

Plugin Architecture

Extend Aria with first-class provider plugins. YouTube Music, Spotify, and local files out of the box.

Material You

Adaptive theming powered by Material Design 3 — accent colors, light/dark modes, and dynamic wallpaper color on Android 12+.

Synced Lyrics

Follow along line-by-line with time-synced lyrics. Falls back to plain text when timing isn't available.

Offline Downloads

Download tracks and listen anywhere. Queue management and storage tracking built in.

10-Band Equalizer

Fine-tune your sound with a full 10-band EQ. Choose from Flat, Bass Boost, or Treble Boost presets.

Library Management

Organize your entire collection. Browse by songs, albums, artists, and playlists in one cohesive library.

Sleep Timer

Fall asleep to music. Set presets, custom durations, or auto-stop at the end of the current track.

Queue Management

Take control of what plays next. Reorder tracks, shuffle, and set repeat modes on the fly.
